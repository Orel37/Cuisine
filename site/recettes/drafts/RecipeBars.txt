For 6 big bars :
100g oatmeal
100g almonds
200g dried prune
250g rice flakes
37g Whey chocolate
20g honey
tea sp of Cinnamon

Grind the oatmeal and the almonds into flour. Blender the prune. Mix the flour and the prune together until you get a paste. Add cinnamon and mix the rice flakes progressively, until the cereals seems to be well distributed. Then add the whey, honey, and a bit of water (not too much, add progressively too), and mix again. 
Put in a mold, then about half an hour in the freezer.
